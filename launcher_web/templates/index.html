Launch Ecosystem

Starts the Station Engine. The Station will be available on:

  • http://{your-tailscale-ip}:5005/player — Access it from all around the world. You need Tailscale installed; find your IP on the Machines dashboard.
  • http://localhost:5005/player — Only from the main machine.
  • http://{your-local-ip}:5005/player — Only from your home network.

After you click Launch, keep the terminal window where you started this launcher open. That is where the Station Engine runs.
